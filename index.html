Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is a direct download link?

A: A direct download link allows you to download a file instantly without being redirected to the Google Drive preview page.

Q: Is it safe to use this generator?

A: Yes, it's completely safe. We don't store your Google Drive links or files.

Q: My link isn't working. What should I do?
